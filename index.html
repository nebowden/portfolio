about me

Hi, I'm Nathan. I'm a web developer in Austin, Texas, home of bats, blistering heatwaves and festivals for every day of the year. I'm a graduate of Thinkful's Front End Web Development course, through which I learned the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery and AJAX.

Before I began coding, I had a 15-year career as a lawyer, having worked 8 of those years in-house for a large technology company in the Austin area. After 15 years as a lawyer, I began to want something new, different and creative, and found that web development was a great fit for the part of me that loved the logic of law and the part of me that wanted to do something a little more creative.

In my spare time, I like to play guitar, cook, take photographs, draw, read all sorts of books from thrillers to sci-fi to non-fiction, listen to comedy podcasts and go to live comedy shows. I also grew up ten minutes from the Texas Rangers' baseball stadium, so I've got an innate love of the team.
